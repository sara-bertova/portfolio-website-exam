03.04.01 Design sprint

Focus?

The purpose of this assignment was to create a native app improving student life at KEA. We were supposed to use the design sprint method for coming up and developing the solution. The prototype had to be high-fidelity, interactive and created in Adobe XD using UI kits to make it as convincing as possible.

My role?

In this project, I was assigned the role of one of the copywriters. I also helped to document each phase of the design sprint by taking pictures and doing screenshots. Moreover, I contributed to the building and final styling of the prototype. In the prototype testing, I acted as a recorder and during the pitch I talked about its insights and planned improvements. Additionally, I put it all together in the final documentation documenting our working process.

New knowledge, perhaps?

In this assignment, I got introduced to the design sprint method and all its phases. I’ve deepened my knowledge concerning copywriting, testing and prototyping in Adobe XD. Moreover, I’ve got a better understanding of how to present our product and communicate our research and test results. In addition, during the pitch deck, I was forced to overcome my fear of public speaking. Finally, we got to use team canvas for the first time which helped us to set the rules and figure out our common goal at the very beginning of the project.
