04.04.01 Link to the Onboarding

Focus?

The purpose was to create an interactive, animated, web-based onboarding sequence. It was supposed to use gamification to explain a certain aspect of being a non-Dane who moved to Denmark to start their studies at KEA Multimedia Design programme. I worked in a group of 4 and we decided to focus on the extracurricular activities at different KEA campuses.

My role?

During the introductory decisions we made as a group I was assigned the role of a Decider – an honour and a curse. One of my other tasks was to create a persona collage about a fictive person representing our target audience. Furthermore, I managed to do the paper sketches of our characters, After School Club logo and a gif in Photoshop. Since I was in the group where no one had a close relationship to coding, I also found myself being the one dealing with HTML, CSS and Javascript.

New knowledge, perhaps?

First of all, I got familiar with all the aspects of multimedia development. I improved my coding skills and overcame my fear of paper sketching. I found out about a persona collage and how it can actually help you to get a clearer image of your target audience. I’ve learnt how to make an image trace in Illustrator and create a gif animation in Photoshop.

Moreover, I discovered that there are certain criteria for posting a gif on different social platforms. Peer review was also new to me but I realized it is a good way of getting professional feedback. Afterwards, you can build on that and start planning future improvements. Lastly, it was the first time I did such an exhibition and saw the people interact with our product.
