05.02.03 Storyboard

Focus?

This task was a part of the Foodsharing Copenhagen campaign assignment and its goal was to prepare our video production. The storyboard which is basically documentation including all the shots in the planned video was supposed to visually guide us throughout the production process.

My role?

As a group, we went through the ideation process. We came up with multiple ideas for our campaign video and discussed each of them carefully – pros and cons. After deciding in which direction we wanted to go we prepared our treatment and were finally ready to dive into our storyboard. While making it we thought about the different shots, sounds and props as well as about the composition which would fit the best our story.

New knowledge, perhaps?

I’ve acquired knowledge regarding film language, dramaturgy and dramaturgical elements. I’ve learnt what the storyboard actually is and what it should contain. Moreover, I’ve understood its crucial role in the production process since it helps us save time and energy, test our video before we move to the actual filming and basically assures us we are all on the same page.
